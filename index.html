ABOUT

Hi! My name is Claire and I and a Games Programmer with a degree of Bachelor of Information Technology (Games and Graphics Programming) from RMIT and a Bachelor of Arts (Digital Design) from Curtin University. My love for games and the industry began many years ago; my family were living on Christmas Island and my friend received the game Kingdom Hearts on Playstation 2 for her birthday. We spent every day after school for about 2 weeks, they way that I felt playing is something I wont forget and it inspired me - if I could make even one person feel the way I felt that day well thats the dream.

I believe myself quite adaptable to different learning styles and pride myself on asking questions and getting guidance to ensure that the work I am producing is what is needed and that of a high quality. I thrive in a collaborative, friendly and supporting environment. I enjoy work that allows me to utilize my knowledge and skills, and face challenges that provide me an opportunity to learn and grow.

I currently work at Immutable where I contribute to the free-to-play tactical card game; Gods Unchained
