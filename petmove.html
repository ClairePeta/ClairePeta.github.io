Pet.Move()

Pet.Move() is a game of survival, be the last animal standing to win.

You control your character via the use of logic elements, which are stored in your inventory. You have a maximum of four moves to make each turn, as the game progresses you can collect new logic blocks. Use these logic blocks to create your strategy, you can push amd pull players into traps of orr the edge. Watch your lives as you only get three, evade traps and your fellow racers to be the last survivor
